Our Vision

We believe that software should support humanity in three areas: evolution, entertainment, and education. To intensify our efforts, Zenware has established 3 subbrands, each focusing on one of these purposes.

Evolution

Software is key in helping society move forward, and the co-existence between humans and computers become more vital with each passing day. Therefore, we must ensure that the programs written today are build to last. Based on first-hand experience with more than 10 programming languages, Zenware Solutions centers its development around the "clean code" principles, which ensures that code is both functional and maintainable. Combined with in-depth knowledge of a large range of tools and frameworks, we are able to deliver the high-quality software that customers need.

Education

The Digital Revolution has radically changed the skills and knowledge needed to navigate daily life. At Zenware Academy, we hope to prepare society for these changes, by creating educational information about IT and technology - all freely available to the public.

Entertainment

What would life be without play? Zenware Games aims to develop and publish video-games, which keep the players' feelings (and not their wallets) in focus, and brings captivating enjoyment to your digital appliances.
